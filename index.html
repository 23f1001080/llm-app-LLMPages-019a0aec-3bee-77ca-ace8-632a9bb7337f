Welcome to My GitHub Pages Site!

This site hosts a collection of files, each created to demonstrate various skills and thought processes as part of a development task.

Contents:

  • ashravan.txt: A 300-400 word short story in the style of Brandon Sanderson, exploring what happens to Ashravan after his restoration by Shai.
  • dilemma.json: An ethical thought experiment on autonomous vehicle decision-making in a moral dilemma, presented in JSON format.
  • about.md: A brief, three-word description of myself.
  • pelican.svg: An SVG graphic depicting a pelican riding a bicycle.
  • restaurant.json: A recommendation for a good restaurant in Mumbai, including its name, location coordinates, and suggested dishes, in JSON format.
  • prediction.json: A prediction for the Federal Funds rate by December 2025, along with the reasoning, in JSON format.
  • uid.txt: An important unique identifier string.
  • LICENSE: The MIT License under which this project is distributed.

Feel free to browse the files and explore their content!
